Darth Vader

The right hand of the Emperor and the most feared Jedi Hunter the Galaxy has ever known. Darth Vader is the terrifying Sith Lord that enforces the laws of the Galactic Empire. Easily recognizable by his haunting dark visage and chilling rythmic breathing, this Sith Lord strikes fear into all who bear witness to his presence. If you ever have the misfortune of seeing him, do not fight, run as fast as you can and hope that he does not deem you worthy of pursuit.

>

Darth Vader is a prominant figure in the Star Wars universe created by director George Lucas. People were first introduced to the Sith Lord in Star Wars Episode IV: A New Hope where he was portrayed by David Prowse and voiced by James Earl Jones and went on to appear in Episode V and Episode VI. Since then he has been the subject of many comics, books, and even recently appeared in the Star Wars TV adaptation: Star Wars Rebels.
